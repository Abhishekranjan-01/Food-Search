How It Works

  1. Click On The Button "Search By Nutrients".
  2. Then Tick The Metioned Nutrients Which You Want In Your Food Suggestions. (On Tick, By Default It Will Take 10g On Per 100g Serving).
  3. If You Want Specific Amount Of Nutrients Then You Can Click To Nutrient's Name,After That a small input box will pop-up and there you can input MIN and MAX amount of nutrient on PER 100g Serving. (OPTIONAL)
  4. After All Above Steps,You Can Click On Submit Button.
  5. After That You Will Get The Food Suggestions Based On Your Selection,Where Selected Nutrient Will Have Light Green Bakcground with their amount.
  6. Else,If Such Selected Amount Related Food Does Not Exist On The API's database (The Alert Message Will POP-UP regarding that)
